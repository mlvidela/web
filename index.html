About Me

I’m from Viedma (Patagonia), where I grew up curious and dreaming big, always eager to create positive change. Now based in Buenos Aires, I combine my passion for education, leadership, and technology with a practical, agile approach. Work as a programming workshop coordinator and tutor, having designed and led learning experiences for over 1,000 students aged 15 to 18. I teach Python, JavaScript, and web development with a creative and inclusive mindset, and I lead initiatives like Mujeres Digitales to promote female participation in tech. Also, coordinate high-performing teams in public service environments, applying agile methodologies to deliver effective, collaborative results. Currently, I am pursuing a Technical Degree in Data Analysis at UBA, developing technical skills in SQL, Power BI, and GitHub, alongside my experience in leadership and project management. I’m passionate about turning complex data into clear insights that drive strategic decisions and creating spaces where people can grow, learn, and trust their own path.
