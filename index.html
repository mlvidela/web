About Me

I grew up in Viedma (Patagonia), where I learned to see the world with curiosity and to dream big. I live in Buenos Aires now, but that root is still alive in me—it drives me to explore, to keep learning, and to seek ways to create meaningful impact.

I've been dancing since I was very young—almost since I learned to walk—and that form of expression shaped the way I move through the world: with sensitivity, discipline, and passion. Later, I found new languages in education, technology, and innovation—powerful tools to turn ideas into action.

I’m curious, multifaceted, and a strong believer in the power of self-directed learning.

I’m inspired by helping others discover what they’re capable of when they trust themselves—especially when they’re given space to explore, to make mistakes, and to try again.
